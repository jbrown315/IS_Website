Timeline

What is Artificial Intelligence?

I began the year by identifying the term Artificial Intelligence and what different things in the real world fall under its umbrella.

What is an Artificial Neural Network?

After identifying some important vocabulary, I went on to explore the concept of a Neural Network. Exploring what it is and how it relates to a Biological Neural Network.

What is a Perceptron?

After learning about ANNs in general, I went on to focus on a Perceptron, the simplest form of an Artificial Neural Network. I learned how they work and how to physically build one.

The Working Model

This is where I spent the majority of my time this year. Working on developing a working Perceptron model, and then advancing it to work on the website. I then went on to increase it to three inputs, and ultimately nine for the COVID-19 Example.
